🔒 Privacy Policy

Short version: We don't collect any personal data. Everything happens in your browser.

What we don't collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone)
  • No account or signup
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No third-party trackers or ads
  • No fingerprinting

What stays on your device

  • Your scripts, titles, and generated videos — never uploaded anywhere
  • Your face/voice uploads — processed locally in your browser, never sent to a server
  • Your theme preference (light/dark) — stored in localStorage on your device only

Network requests we make

The only external requests are:

  • Picsum Photos (picsum.photos) — to fetch free background images. No personal data is sent.
  • Pixabay / YouTube — these open in a new tab only when you click a music link.

Cookies

ScriptSpark does not use cookies. We use localStorage only to remember your theme preference (light/dark/system) on your device. This is never sent to any server.

Children's privacy

ScriptSpark is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.
