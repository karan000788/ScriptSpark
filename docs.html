How to Use Creatora

A complete guide to generating viral YouTube scripts and thumbnails with AI — for free.

What is Creatora?

Creatora is a free AI-powered script & thumbnail studio for YouTube creators. It creates:

  • Viral video ideas — AI-generated trending topics for your niche
  • Retention-optimized scripts — 400-600 words, Hook, Problem, Story, Reveal, CTA
  • Custom thumbnails — AI-generated 1280x720 images via Pollinations.ai
  • YouTube titles — Click-worthy, high-CTR titles

Free, no signup, no watermarks. Uses your own free Groq API key. Runs entirely in your browser.

Step-by-Step Guide

1 Pick Your Niche

Choose from 15 content categories: Dark Mystery, Finance, Motivation, Tech, True Crime, History, Astrology, Health, Gaming, Relationships, Science, Food, Travel, Business, Mythology.

AI will generate trending video ideas tailored to your chosen niche.

2 Choose from 5 Viral Ideas

AI generates 5 viral video ideas with titles and reasons why they'll perform well. Click any idea to proceed to script generation.

3 Review Your Script

A 400-600 word retention-optimized script is generated with:

  • Hook — First 3 seconds that stop the scroll
  • Problem — The pain point your audience relates to
  • Story — The narrative build-up
  • Reveal — The solution or twist
  • CTA — Subscribe and follow call to action

Copy the script or proceed to thumbnail generation.

4 Generate & Download Thumbnail

AI creates a detailed image prompt based on your script, then generates a 1280x720 thumbnail image via Pollinations.ai. Download it directly or regenerate for a new variation.

Tips for Viral Videos

  • Be specific with your niche. "Dark Psychology" works better than "Psychology".
  • First 1 second matters most. Our hooks are designed to stop the scroll.
  • Use emotion in titles. Words like "secret", "truth", "shocking", "mistake" boost CTR.
  • Post 2-3 videos/week. Consistency beats perfection.
  • Always end with a clear CTA. Like + Subscribe + Comment is the proven formula.
  • Write in English or Hindi — the two largest YouTube audiences in India.

FAQ

Is Creatora really free?

Yes. You need a free Groq API key (get one in 30 seconds at console.groq.com). Thumbnail generation via Pollinations.ai is also free. No signup, no credit card, no hidden costs.

Does my data leave my browser?

No. Your Groq API key and all content stay in your browser. The only network requests are:

  • Groq API — for AI text generation (your key, your request)
  • Pollinations.ai — for thumbnail image generation (no key needed)

Nothing is sent to our servers because we have no servers.

Which languages are supported?

English and Hindi. Scripts can be generated in either language. The Groq API handles both well.

How do I get a Groq API key?

Go to console.groq.com/keys, sign up (free), and create an API key. Paste it in Settings. It takes about 30 seconds.

What format is the thumbnail?

1280x720 pixels (YouTube standard thumbnail size). Generated as a PNG image via Pollinations.ai.

Which browsers are supported?

Latest Chrome, Edge, Brave, or Firefox on desktop. Mobile works too, but desktop is recommended for the best experience.
