📖 How to Use ScriptSpark

A complete guide to generating viral YouTube videos in your language — for free.

🚀 Open the App

🎯 What is ScriptSpark?

ScriptSpark.in is a free AI-powered YouTube video generator built for Indian creators. It creates:

  • 🧠 Viral titles with emojis in 10 Indian languages
  • 📝 Retention-optimised scripts with a 1-second hook
  • 🎵 Copyright-free music suggestions (Pixabay + YouTube Audio Library)
  • 🎥 Downloadable videos (Shorts 60s or Long, custom length)
  • 🔊 Voiceover in your language (Web Speech API)
  • 🙂 Optional face overlay & voice sample

100% free, no signup, no API keys, no watermarks. Everything runs in your browser.

🚀 Step-by-Step Guide

1 Pick Your Language

Choose from 10 Indian languages: English, Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Marathi, Gujarati, Punjabi, Malayalam, Kannada.

The script, title, and voiceover will all be generated in the language you pick.

2 Enter Your Topic

Type your video topic — be specific. Examples:

  • Dark Psychology
  • Indian Stock Market for Beginners
  • Street Food of Mumbai
  • AI Tools for Students

3 Tell Us More (the secret to great scripts)

Briefly describe what the video should cover. The more specific, the better the output. You can also:

  • Write a brief (e.g. "3 dark psychology tricks that make people buy things")
  • Provide your own title (and we'll generate a script around it)
  • Specify the audience (e.g. "Indian teens 16-22")

4 Choose Format & Length

YouTube Shorts (60s) or Long Video (1-60 min).

5 Pick a Viral Idea

5 viral title ideas in your language. If you provided your own title, it appears as the first option.

6 Script + Customise

Retention-optimised script (built from your brief), copyright-free music, and optional customisation:

  • Real Videos as background (Pexels API) or Photos / Animated / Solid
  • Your face overlay
  • Your voice sample as ambient voice
  • Voice picker in Settings

7 Edit Scenes New

Scene-by-scene editor. You can:

  • Edit any scene's text
  • Regenerate a single scene's text
  • Reorder scenes (drag with arrows)
  • Delete unwanted scenes
  • Add new scenes
  • Adjust each scene's duration in seconds
  • Everything is auto-saved to your browser

8 Render & Download Your Video

Click Render Video. We pre-render all frames (so it's 5-10x faster than real-time), then encode to .webm.

When it's done, you can:

  • Play with Voiceover — watch + hear the script in your language
  • Regenerate Video
  • Download Script
  • Download .webm

💡 Tips for Viral Videos

  • Be specific with your niche. "Dark Psychology" works better than "Psychology".
  • First 1 second matters most. Our hooks are designed to stop the scroll.
  • Use emotion in titles. Words like "secret", "truth", "shocking", "mistake" boost CTR.
  • Pattern break every 7-8 sec. Our scripts do this automatically via scene changes.
  • Post 2-3 videos/week. Consistency beats perfection.
  • Always end with a clear CTA. Like + Subscribe + Comment is the proven formula.
  • Use the suggested music — they're all copyright-free, safe for monetisation.
  • Post in your language — India has 500M+ Hindi, 90M+ Tamil, 80M+ Telugu speakers hungry for content.

❓ FAQ

Is ScriptSpark really free?

Yes — 100% free, no signup, no API keys, no hidden costs, no watermarks. You can use the videos you generate commercially.

Does my data leave my browser?

No. Everything runs in your browser. The only network requests are:

  • Picsum (background photos) — free image service, no personal data sent
  • Pixabay / YouTube (music links) — links you click open in a new tab

Your face/voice uploads, scripts, and videos stay on your device.

Can it clone my voice?

Not yet — true neural voice cloning needs paid AI models. The free alternative we use is your browser's Web Speech API, which reads the script in your language using the OS's native voice.

If you upload a 30-60 sec voice sample, it plays softly in the background as your "voice signature" (looped at low volume). It gives the video a personal feel without using paid AI.

Which browsers are supported?

Latest Chrome, Edge, Brave, or Firefox on desktop. Mobile Chrome/Safari works too, but desktop is recommended for best video quality.

Web Speech API voices vary by browser & OS — Windows, macOS, Android, and iOS each have different built-in voices for Indian languages.

Can I make YouTube Shorts with this?

Yes! Choose YouTube Shorts in Step 3. The video will be rendered in 9:16 vertical format, exactly 60 seconds, perfect for Shorts.

How long does video rendering take?

Roughly equal to the video length:

  • 60s Shorts — ~60s to render
  • 5 min video — ~5 min to render
  • 10 min video — ~10 min to render

Rendering speed depends on your device. The progress bar shows real-time status.

What format is the downloaded video?

.webm (VP9 + Opus). It plays in all modern browsers, on YouTube, and in most video editors. You can convert to MP4 with free tools like HandBrake if needed.

Is the music really copyright-free?

Yes. We link to two 100% royalty-free sources:

  • Pixabay Music — free for commercial use, no attribution required
  • YouTube Audio Library — free for YouTube creators, safe for monetisation

You should still download them separately and add them to your video in your editor for the best quality.

Which Indian languages are supported?

10 languages: English, Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Marathi, Gujarati, Punjabi, Malayalam, Kannada.

Voiceover quality depends on your device — Windows/macOS/Android ship with native voices for most of these.

🛠️ Free Tools We Use

ScriptSpark is built entirely with free, browser-native technology:

  • Picsum Photos — free stock photos, no API key needed
  • Pixabay Music — free royalty-free music
  • YouTube Audio Library — free music for creators
  • Web Speech API — built into every modern browser
  • HTML5 Canvas — for video frame rendering
  • MediaRecorder API — for capturing canvas + audio to .webm
  • Web Audio API — for music synthesis & sound effects
  • System fonts — no Google Fonts, no paid fonts

No third-party trackers, no ads, no analytics, no cookies (except your theme preference).

🔒 Security & Privacy

ScriptSpark runs entirely in your browser. Here's how your data is protected:

  • No server, no tracking — there is no backend; your ideas, scripts and videos stay on your device.
  • Content Security Policy — strict CSP blocks unauthorized scripts and external resources.
  • Encrypted Pexels API key — your key is stored in IndexedDB encrypted with AES-GCM (256-bit), and the encryption key never leaves your browser.
  • EXIF stripped from uploads — face images are re-encoded through canvas, so GPS / camera data is removed before storage.
  • Magic-number file validation — uploaded files are checked by their actual bytes, not just the file extension.
  • No third-party trackers — no ads, no analytics, no cookies (except your theme preference).
  • Frame-ancestors none — no other site can embed this app in an iframe (clickjacking protection).
